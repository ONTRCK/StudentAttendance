ONTRCK is your centralised attendance tracker. Follow the three steps below to sign in and access attendance application.

How to Sign In
1
Enter your NWU student e-mail address

Use your eight-digit student number followed by @mynwu.ac.za

✉ 12345678@mynwu.ac.za
2
Select "Active Directory" as the sign-in method

When prompted, choose Active Directory from the available authentication options before entering your password.

3
Log in with your CAS password

Use the same password you use for eFundi. This is your Central Authentication Service (CAS) password shared across all NWU systems.

💡
Forgotten your CAS password? Reset it at my.nwu.ac.za using the self-service portal, or contact the NWU IT Helpdesk for assistance.
